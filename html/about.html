Our Objective

Our main objective is to reach out to our communities and make the process of healing easily accessible
to everyone. We provide an online platform so that users can easily search and
book appointments, to their nearest rehabilitation centers in their community.
​ In addition, we want to prevent and help treat the alcohol and drug dependencies,
by making it easier for people to find all the rehabilitation centers near them.
